LiveRamp

LiveRamp is a data connectivity platform operating mostly in the ad-tech space. At LiveRamp I am a Java platform engineer. I've Built an API using Spring to define a platform boundary which enabled new business verticals to leverage an existing service. My team manages a data pipeline distributing 200 TB/day to over 500 integrations. I also build and maintained integrations with platforms to distribute data at scale.

Zephair

Zephair does drone photography and cinematography, with a current focus in high-end building construction, both finished and under construction. FAA UAS (Unmanned Aircraft Systems) certified. Full service from start to end with photo and video editing. Currently contracting with Albaugh Masonry Stone and Tile and have photographed 21 sites.

Wineman Technology

Wineman Technology is a system integrator specializing in custom equipment to provide insight through electromechanical testing. While there, I learned LabVIEW software development from certified LabVIEW Architects, as well as gaining experience with Veristand and serial communication.

Delta Network Services

Delta Network Services installs full network systems from network switches to phone systems to security systems. Delta searches for the best products and works to integrate them together. I was responsible for network switch configuration, installation and testing, as well as working closely with engineers on network system design.

Rapid Prototype Company

Rapid Prototype Company specializes in producing prototypes and small production runs. A majority of the parts are created using stereolithography, with the small production runs using silicon molds. I worked mostly with designing the support structures and cleaning the final parts.

Dynamic Robotics Solutions

DRS manufactures turnkey 3D robotic cutting and trimming systems for the plastics, automotive, heavy truck, construction, marine, bath & spa and home appliance industries. With their advanced robotics expertise, DRS is the global leader in robotic process automation for flexible material removal systems. While there, my biggest project was designing a sensor mount that is now included in their advance sensor calibration package.

Chase Plastics

Chase Plastics is a leader in global resin plastics distribution. I interned there as an assistant to the information technology manager. The largest project that I worked on while there was a digital sign-in system with a centralized database, for increased convenience and enabling improved tracking of employees in case of emergency. It was created with an SQL database in the back end, and HTML and PHP in the front end.
