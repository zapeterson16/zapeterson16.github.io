Juggling

I am a member of the Jugglers On Campus at U of M. My favorite types of juggling are passing pins and working on numbers; I can juggle 4 right now and am working on juggling 5.

I learned to juggle balls from my mom, and learned to do pins and pass at the Motor City Juggling Club at Oakland University. I've juggled at a few charity events and was a guest actor as a jester in a play.
