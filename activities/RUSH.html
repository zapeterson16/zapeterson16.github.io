Team RUSH Robotics

FIRST is an organization that helps to promote science and technology. The First Robotics Competition is a competition where a game is released in january then teams build a 120 pound robot over the course of 6 weeks then compete.

About Team RUSH

Team RUSH is a FIRST robotics team competing in the FIRST robotics competition. Team Rush is one of the oldest teams in FIRST with over 20 years of experience. in 2013 Team RUSH won the Chairman's award which is the highest award in all of FIRST robotics. Team RUSH was also the state champion in 2014 and 2016, being a state finalist in 2015. Team RUSH was also a finalist in it's division at the world competition in 2014 and 2015. I was one of the two robot operators from 2014-2016.

My Contribution

Being at Team RUSH was how I spent almost all my free time in high school. For me during build season it was almost every day except sunday from after school until 9 and from 9 to 4 on saturday. When I started I jumped in on the programming sub-team and was the back-up robot operator freshman year. My sophomore year I took on the scouting app and it was our most successful scouting app. I also took on more of the software development my sophomore year and was the robot operator for competitions. My junior year I was the captain of the Programming sub-team and developed a majority of the software for the robot in LabVIEW and was still the robot operator. My senior year I was the team captain and wrote all of the software for the robot with help from Wineman Technology on the autonomous software. Find out more about that here. My senior year I was also the robot operator for the last time.
