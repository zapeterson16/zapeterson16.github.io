Scouting App

At FIRST robotics competitions having data about the robots you are playing with and against is very important. Team RUSH is very serious about data collection, we collect data on every robot during every match. To handle this type of active data collection the scouting app was born.

The App

The app was developed in Ojective C and ran on 6 ipads at the competition. During each match a scouter was assigned to a robot. The app created a CSV file for each robot in each match and could parse through those so that you could view previous matches. The data stored in CSV files was synced with a computer using an excel macro that compiled the data, from there the data was analyzed with tableu. I was responsible for developing the app and getting the data from the app to the computer. Check out the finished app below. Each year we also printed paper scouting sheets in case the app didn't function like it was supposed to. The year I created the app was the first year that we didn't have to revert to scouting on paper during any matches.
